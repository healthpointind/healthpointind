Privacy Policy

Effective: 15 March 2025
Pune, Maharashtra, India

1. Information We Collect

For booking diagnostic tests (CT Scan, MRI, Blood Tests etc.), we collect:

  • Patient Name
  • Contact Number (WhatsApp enabled)
  • Email Address (optional)
  • Preferred Test Type
  • Selected Lab Location in Pune

2. Data Usage

Your information is used to:

  • Book appointments at NABL-accredited labs in Pune
  • Share test reports via WhatsApp/Email
  • Send SMS reminders for appointments
  • Improve our service (anonymous analytics)

3. Data Sharing

We share information with:

  • Partner labs in Pune area
  • Google Analytics (anonymized data)
  • Meta Pixel (for ad performance tracking)

4. Data Security

We follow ISO 27001 standards with:

  • SSL encrypted form submissions
  • 2FA protected Google Sheets storage
  • Regular security audits

5. Your Rights (Under IT Act 2000)

  • Access your data via email request
  • Request deletion by contact us
  • Opt-out of marketing messages

6. Children's Privacy

For patients under 18, we require parent/guardian consent.

7. Updates

Policy changes notified via website banner
