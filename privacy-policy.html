Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15 March 2025
Last Updated: 15 March 2025

Welcome to Health Point IND. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website or interact with our services. By using our platform, you consent to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

To facilitate diagnostic test bookings (CT Scan, MRI, Blood Tests, etc.), we collect:

  • Personal Information: Full name, WhatsApp-enabled phone number, email address (optional)
  • Service Details: Preferred test type, selected lab location in Pune
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage patterns via cookies

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data enables us to:

  • Book appointments at NABL-accredited labs
  • Deliver test reports via WhatsApp/Email
  • Send appointment reminders via SMS
  • Personalize your experience and improve services
  • Analyze website traffic and ad campaign performance using third-party tools

3. Data Sharing & Partners

We share information with trusted third parties to deliver our services:

  • Service Providers: Partner labs in Pune for appointment fulfillment
  • Analytics & Advertising:
    • Google Analytics (anonymized usage data)
    • Meta Pixel (for ad targeting and performance measurement)

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Enhance website functionality
  • Analyze traffic patterns
  • Deliver personalized ads on platforms like Google and Facebook

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Opting out may affect website functionality.

5. Data Security

We protect your information using:

  • ISO 27001-aligned security protocols
  • SSL encryption for all form submissions
  • Two-factor authentication for data storage systems
  • Regular security audits and access controls

6. Your Rights

Under Indian IT Act 2000 and GDPR principles, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections or deletion
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Object to automated decision-making

Contact us via the details below to exercise these rights.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18. We require verifiable parental consent for any minor's data collection.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy to reflect changes in practices or regulations. Significant updates will be communicated via:

  • Website banner notifications
  • Email alerts (for registered users)
